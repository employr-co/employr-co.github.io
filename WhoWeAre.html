Meet Our Team

People dedicated to helping you.

Christopher Bazela
President, Founder

Chris Bazela is a Sophomore at Bucknell University currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Economics. Chris is a member of the Bucknell Track has Field team where he competes in the shotput and discus. In his free time, Chris enjoys fishing, working out, and spending time with family and friends.

After deciding to study remotely for the semester, Chris wanted to get a job on top of a fairly busy schedule with school and training for track. After many different applications, scheduling became a common deterrent from obtaining a job. This is where Employr was born. Chris wanted to create an efficient application for both businesses and job-seekers to find the job not only best for their skills but for their schedule. Too often applications, phone calls, and interviews are completed just to find out that the scheduling did not align. It has been his mission to create a better system for job search/recruiting.  

Andrew Campi
Developer

Andrew Campi is a rising Sophomore at Bucknell University currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science. Andrew is a tech enthusiast, completing a dozen personal computer programming projects. In addition to technology, Andrew has been a passionate drummer for over eight years, playing in the Bucknell University Jazz Ensemble. He is a graduate of Christian Brother's Academy in 2019.

Andrew has a constant drive for knowledge, and always wants to learn something new. His creative side drives him to start new and challenging projects, and his organizational side enables him to see them to completion.
