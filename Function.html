How It Works

Employr is a user generated application. Our integrated software system allows users to input their availability which then connects them with businesses whose hours fit their needs.

Employr is beneficial because users can essentially “create their own hours” before going through the job application process. They will be given results of the type of jobs available within a desired radius for the specific amount of time in which they choose.
